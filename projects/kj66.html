KJ66 Micro Turbojet CAD

CATIA CAD Jet Engine

Created a full CAD model of the KJ66 model jet turbine engine from dimension sheets and improved several components to boost performance. Redesigned parts include hydrodynamic bearings, cowl, removable turbine stator vanes, exhaust cone struts, mounting brackets, compressor diffuser, and shaft housing.

Published at the ASEE EDGD Midyear Conference, 2016.
